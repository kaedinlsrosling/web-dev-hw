Napkin Folding

I choose this project because it's effortless, but also added decoration to a dull, boring dinner table while also serving a purpose.

This project is unique to me because family dinners are significant in my family, and being able to put a little extra effort into them means a lot to me.

You can learn many other different ways to fold napkins at TasteofHome.com.
