How to Fold a Napkin:

Place Card Holder Fold

This fold is not only functional, but it also adds an elegant touch to any table.


Step 1:

Fold a napkin in half twice to make a square and place it on a flat surface with the open corners facing you. Fold up each open corner one at a time, placing each corner about one inch from the previous one.


Step 2:

Fold up the bottom edge about an inch to make a holder for the place card.


Step 3:

Turn napkin over and fold napkin in thirds, tucking one corner under the other to hold.


Step 4:

Turn the napkin over and put place card inside the bottom fold.
