Data travels through the internet in packets, which are directed by an IP address. Packets travel from one machine to another until they reach there destination.
An IP address is an address given to a computer to identify it on a network. A DNS is used to convert the domain name used by humans into IP addresses for the computers.
Cookies are files that are on your computers that are created when you visit a website. They store information from your visit of that site and web servers send them to your web browser.
